Japanese Quality Living in the Heart of Jakarta

Finding the right home is about more than location—it is about quality, comfort, and a lifestyle
that feels effortless. BRANZ Mega Kuningan brings together Japanese-inspired craftsmanship
and modern urban living, offering residents a premium residential experience in one of Jakarta’s
most prestigious business districts.

Developed by Tokyu Land Indonesia, BRANZ reflects the values commonly associated with
Japanese residential design: attention to detail, functional layouts, quality construction, and
long-term comfort. Every aspect of the residence has been thoughtfully planned to create a
living environment that is both elegant and practical for today’s professionals and families.

Located in the heart of Mega Kuningan, BRANZ offers excellent connectivity to Jakarta’s major
business hubs, including Sudirman, SCBD, and Gatot Subroto. Multinational offices, embassies,
luxury hotels, shopping centers, and fine dining destinations are all within easy reach, allowing
residents to enjoy a convenient and well-balanced lifestyle.

Inside the residence, spacious interiors and carefully designed layouts maximize natural light
and create a welcoming atmosphere. High-quality finishes, premium materials, and
sophisticated architecture combine to deliver a home that is both stylish and comfortable.

Residents also enjoy an impressive selection of facilities designed to support everyday well-
being. From a fully equipped fitness center and swimming pool to a sauna, landscaped gardens, and elegant residents’ lounge, every space has been created to encourage relaxation and
healthy living. Professional building management and 24-hour security further enhance the
overall living experience.

For Japanese expatriates and international professionals, BRANZ offers a familiar sense of
quality while embracing the energy of Jakarta’s dynamic city life. The surrounding neighborhood
features a variety of international restaurants, cafés, supermarkets, healthcare facilities, and
lifestyle destinations, making everyday living both convenient and enjoyable.

At BRANZ Mega Kuningan, Japanese-inspired quality is reflected not only in the building itself
but also in the overall living experience. Combining exceptional design, premium facilities, and a
prime location, it offers a refined urban lifestyle for those seeking comfort, convenience, and
lasting value in the heart of Jakarta.
